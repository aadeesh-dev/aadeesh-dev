# 🌐 Adding a Published Application Route

Follow this guide to configure and add a published application route via your Zero Trust dashboard. 

## 📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Access the Dashboard** 
   From the main sidebar, navigate to the **Zero Trust Dashboard**.

2. **Navigate to Networks** 
   In the dashboard menu, click on **Networks**.

3. **Open Connectors** 
   Under the Networks section, select **Connectors**.

4. **Select Your Tunnel** 
   Locate and click on the specific tunnel or connector where your application is currently hosted.

5. **Go to Published Routes** 
   On the tunnel's details page, look at the top menu bar (just below the main navigation/path bar) and select the **Published application routes** tab.

6. **Add the Route** 
   Click the blue **Add a published application route** button to configure your new route.
